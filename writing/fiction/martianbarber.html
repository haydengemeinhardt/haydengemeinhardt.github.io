The Martian Barber

From the viewpoint of a barber on Mars, humanity is beginning to look at colonizing the nearby star system of Alpha Centauri, 4.37 light years away. Cryo Corp’s new breakthrough in technology is propelling the speed of travel, and is looking at creating an utopian society far from the reaches of anyone else.

This is my attempt at exploring the future of technology, politics, and culture, and other philosophical ideas. I try to make it seem realistic, though that is not only impossible to accomplish, but beyond my realm of knowledge that would take years of research to become an expert in. I could avoid those problems as a whole, but I’d rather make myself look a fool than shy away from writing to the best of my ability.
