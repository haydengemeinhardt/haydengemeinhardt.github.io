Updates To Website


By: Hayden Gemeinhardt | November 29, 2020


My first major update since I created this website on github! Which is also the reason I haven't had a post in a bit.

For the most noticable one, I changed the style of the pages to look less... lackluster? I'm not sure which word to use, but it looks a lot better. I kept the old style with things like blogs just because I find it easier to read.

There is now also a research tab! I'm going to try to document all of the work I've done in research at LSU, starting from when I joined the DeepDrug team back in October.

A smaller change, but one I'm glad about, is the memory game is now fixed. I am still not sure why it was (quite literally) randomly breaking to be honest, but upon fixing the issue, I was surprised it worked in the first place. It only took me a month or two to figure out haha.....

Anywho, I finally registered the domain with Google's search engine. Supposedly. I still need to work out a couple of kinks, but that will come after finals right around the corner here.

That's all I have for now. I am really enjoying the way this website is turning out. It has exceeded expectations already!
